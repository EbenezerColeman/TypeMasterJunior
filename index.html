About TypeMaster Junior

TypeMaster Junior is an educational typing game designed for Junior High School students to improve their typing speed and accuracy. The app uses real-time feedback, scoring, sound effects, and visual cues to make learning interactive and fun.

Developed by Ebenezer Badu Coleman as part of a multimedia project for teacher education.
